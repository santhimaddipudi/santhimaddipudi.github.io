# about

About me

I am a AI and Data Analytics professional with a strong foundation in Python, SQL and R. My main specialities include Analytics, AI + ML, and Data Engineering, combined with practical experience working on real-world problems. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computational Data Sciences from The Penn State University and have worked in AI & Analytics-focused roles across internships and full-time positions.

My work spans large-scale data processing, exploratory data analysis, and AI+ML-driven insights, with projects in domains such as traffic analytics, public health (COVID-19), and algorithmic decision-making systems. I am particularly interested in the intersection of technology, decision-making, and management, and I am always seeking opportunities that offer international exposure.
